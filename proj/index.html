ABOUT IIT(ISM) DHANBAD

IIT (ISM) stands as a fully residential technical institute equipped with modern amenities, situated in the mineral-rich belt of India in the city of Dhanbad, Jharkhand. Established in 1926, it drew inspiration from the Royal School of Mines, London. Originally conceived to provide mining education, it has evolved into an internationally acclaimed technical institution. Presently, it offers a diverse range of programs, including B. Tech, M. Tech, Integrated M. Tech., M.Sc., MBA, and Ph.D. Admission for undergraduate students is conducted through the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE- Advanced), while postgraduate admissions are facilitated through the JAM/GATE entrance exams.

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Mathematics and Computing is known for its outstanding research environment and high-quality teaching, contributing to the academic and industrial success of its students. Over the decades, the department has demonstrated excellence in both teaching and research across core mathematical disciplines and related fields. Academic programs offered include M.Sc. (Mathematics and Computing), Integrated M. Tech (Mathematics and Computing), M. Tech. (Data Analytics), and Ph.D. The department actively participates in sponsored projects from prestigious organizations like CSIR, NBHM, ISRO, DST, with recognition from external funding agencies through SAP-DRS from UGC and FIST from DST. Additionally, the department has established student chapters for the Society of Applied Mathematics (SAM) and the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) to promote academic and research activities, especially among young researchers and students.

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The National Conference provides a collaborative platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to exchange innovative ideas, research findings, and developments. Emphasizing recent advancements in Mathematical Modeling, Analysis, and Simulation, the conference seeks to foster exploration of creative ideas through interactive sessions featuring keynote speakers and participants from all over the country. An additional objective is to inspire young researchers from diverse mathematical domains, acknowledging their contributions through Best Presentation Awards.

Conference Themes

The organizing committee of the conference invites Abstract / Full paper from researchers, academicians and scientists on the major areas which includes but not limited to following:

  • Mathematical Modeling
  • Chaos Modeling
  • Spatial & Fuzzy Modeling
  • Deterministic Modeling
  • Stochastic Modeling
  • Nonlinear Dynamics
  • Numerical Analysis & Simulation
  • Fluid Dynamics
  • Dynamical Systems
  • Mathematical Biology
  • Optimization Techniques
  • Population Balances
  • Continuum Mechanics
  • Statistical Mechanics
  • Differential and Integral Equations
  • ScientificComputing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things
  • Data Science
